               The treatment of the clinic is based on 

         TOTAL QUALITY CONTROL, COMMITMENT TO 

    PATIENTS SATISFACTION, DEDICATION, INTEGRITY,

                                 VALUE AND CARE.

 

 

 

All the patients receive comprehensive care based on the latest techniques, technology, and advanced materials available to the orthotic and prosthetic profession. The clinic takes great pride in 

the exceptional quality of its fitting and design of all devices fabricated  at the OPRRC.We make schedule regular checkups by providing service card, to make sure that the individual orthosis & prosthesis delivers the expected function and comfort to the patient . As a part of comprehensive service we provide periodical adjustments and modifications at certain intervals. These services are performed within the cost containment structure.

 

The main objective and goal of the center is to establish a unique, ideal, clear atmosphere with uncompromising principles orthotic & prosthetic center in central India. The selection of components and materials are strictly based on quality and reliability of the sources. Only the experienced and highly skilled orthotist & prosthetist design ,fabricate and the fit the orthoses and prostheses to the patient as par  the individual needs and requirement.

Each and every member of the center is totally dedicated and devoted to improving lives, welfare and opportunities for the people with physical disabilities. Special trained team of the clinic educated the patients and their concern families member about the use of the particular orthosis and prosthesis, by encouraging them to achieve the identified goals and enjoy the highest possible quality of life.
