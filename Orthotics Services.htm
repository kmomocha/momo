  Total quality, satisfaction and care

 

Orthotics is the Science or field of practice pertaining to prescribe, design, fabrication and fitting, supervised use of orthoses, braces and other device applied to the body to stabilize or immobilize, prevent or correct deformity, protect against injury, promote healing, or assist function, when it is compromised by acute injury, cumulative trauma,disease,surgical intervention, congenital anomaly, or degenerative changes.Orthotist is the person who specialized the Orthotics Science and Orthosis is the product or device.

 

Sometime, in some cases, it is necessary to design and fabricate an orthosis accordingly for a specific need of the patient's dimensions, or incorporates additional components to achieve the desired results. An orthosis is used for a short period until a weakness or injury is overcome, or on a permanent basis to facilitate impaired function due to a congenital or degenerative condition.

 

Special consultation for specialized orthoses is always available for the following area.

 

  • Post polio residual paralysis

  • Postoperative treatment.

  • Chronic condition

  • Fracture bracing

  • Sport orthosis

  • Pediatrics.

  • Geriatrics.
