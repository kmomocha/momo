OPRRC is founded by Momocha Kshetrimayum whose life is totally dedicated to the service and welfare of the orthopaedically disable people.OPRRC, is a private research & rehabilitation centre fully dedicated to the service and welfare of the orthopaedically disable people.OPRRC is involved in developing, manufacturing and fitting of different type of premium qualities orthoses & prostheses to the needy people of the region, right from indigenously made products to the international standard company.

The main ideas behind establishing the center are :

 

1.To provide quality orthotic and prosthetic patient care services in a timely and cost effective manner by highly competent and motivated professionals.

 

2.To create a comfortable environment for the patients, their family, friends and caregivers.

 

3.To recognize and respect the unique aspect of each human spirit and to respond in an interactive and collaborative manner.

 

4.To treat all our patients,doctors,professional associates, and employees in the highest ethical manner, with respect, courtesy, dignity and compassion.
