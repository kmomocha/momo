Total quality, satisfaction and care

Prosthetics is the science or field of practice pertaining to prescribe, design, fabrication and fitting of artificial limbs or any segment of the body who have lost as a result of traumatic injuries, vascular diseases, diabetes, cancer or congenital disorders,  to restore the function and appearance of the lost segment. The fabrication of prosthesis is necessarily an intricate, custom procedure requiring a high degree of skill and sophisticated technology, as there is big difference in the human anatomy. The temporary prosthesis is followed by a definite prosthesis as soon as the residual limb is stable. The person who is qualified or specialized in prosthetics science is known as Prosthetist and the product is called prosthesis. 

 

The design of the prosthesis is determined by the patient's physical changes, level of activity and functional needs. Children need new prostheses on a more frequent basis due to rapid growth rate and high activity level. The adjustment of prosthesis, repairs, realignment, and components up gradation are also aspects of ongoing treatment.
